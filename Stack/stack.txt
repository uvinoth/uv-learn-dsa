Prefix, Infix, and Postfix Conversion:
======================================

What is operator and operand?

Operator:
    ^, *, /, +, -

Operand:
    A to Z
    a to z
    0 to 9


Prefix:
    *+ pq - mm

Infix:
    (p + q) * (m - n)

Postfix:
    pq + mn -*
